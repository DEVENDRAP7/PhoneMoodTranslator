Privacy Policy for Phone Mood Translator

Last updated: January 2026

Phone Mood Translator respects your privacy and is designed to work without collecting personal data.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All analysis is performed locally on your device.

Usage Access Permission

Phone Mood Translator uses Android’s Usage Access permission solely to analyze app usage duration in order to generate mood-based insights for the user.
This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third party.

Data Storage

All mood history and usage-related insights are stored locally on your device. The app does not use cloud storage, external servers, or analytics services.

Third-Party Services

Phone Mood Translator does not use third-party services, advertisements, or trackers.

Children’s Privacy

Phone Mood Translator is not designed for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
